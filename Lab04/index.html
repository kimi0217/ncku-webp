《吉伊卡哇》

這又小又可愛的傢伙

About

Meet Ji Yikawaa (吉伊卡哇)! Ji Yikawaa is a charming and adventurous character known for his unique style and positive spirit. Whether he’s exploring new places or making friends, Ji Yikawaa always brings energy and joy to those around him. With a heart full of curiosity and a smile that lights up the room, Ji Yikawaa is ready for any challenge or adventure that comes his way.

特色照片

Chiikawa

This round, adorable little creature is modeled after a white hamster, featuring a chubby face and big, expressive eyes. With a timid and sensitive personality, it is prone to crying and fussing, yet remains kind-hearted, innocent, and a bit shy. Always striving to improve itself, it often gets troubled by life's little setbacks but never fails to face them with a positive attitude. Its most distinctive trait is that it can only make simple, single-syllable sounds like "wah." As a classic "healing" character, it always brings warmth and comfort to everyone around.

Hachiware

This character is a blue-and-white cat-like animal with a distinctive blue figure-eight marking on its head, pointed ears, and a fringe that gives it a cat-like appearance. Outgoing and lively, it is always full of curiosity and uses a positive attitude to encourage its friends. Known as an eternal optimist, it loves to sing and is great at communication, making it a cheerful and uplifting companion.

Usagi

This character stands out with its long ears and unique, single-lidded eyes. Playful and mischievous, it often acts in unpredictable ways and loves to pull pranks. However, beneath its troublemaking exterior, it genuinely cares about its friends’ feelings and always has their best interests at heart.

Momonga

This character resembles a flying squirrel, complete with a fluffy, expressive tail. Playful and a bit spoiled, it loves to act cute around others and often tries to get everyone’s attention. Its most notable trait is its desire to be the center of attention—always seeking praise and compliments from those around it.
